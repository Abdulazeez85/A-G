The Challenge

Great Cars, Invisible Online

Velocity Motors had a prime location and quality inventory — but zero online presence. When potential buyers searched for cars in their city, competitors with websites were capturing all the interest.

The owner was spending heavily on roadside banners and print ads with diminishing returns. They needed to shift their marketing online — but without a proper website, that was impossible.

The solution had to feel premium enough to match the value of the vehicles on offer, load fast on mobile, and make it effortless for interested buyers to start a conversation via WhatsApp.

What We Built

A Showroom That's Open 24/7

🚗

Vehicle Inventory Pages

Each car has a dedicated detail page with multiple photos, specs, mileage, price, and a WhatsApp enquiry button.

🔍

Search & Filter Inventory

Filter by make, model, year, price range, and fuel type. Buyers find what they want in seconds.

📱

WhatsApp Test Drive Booking

One-tap button on every vehicle page opens WhatsApp with the car details pre-filled — zero friction.

💰

Financing Information

Clear breakdown of financing options, down payment structures, and monthly estimates to reduce buyer hesitation.

🏅

Trust & Credentials Section

Years in business, cars sold, happy customers, and certifications — all the social proof buyers need.

📞

Multiple Contact Channels

Phone, WhatsApp, email, and a contact form — buyers reach the team however they prefer.
