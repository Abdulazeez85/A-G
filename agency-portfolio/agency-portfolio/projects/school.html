The Challenge

A Quality School Losing Students to Competitors With Better Websites

Bright Future Academy had excellent teachers, strong results, and a safe learning environment. But when parents searched for schools online, competitors with more polished websites were winning the enrollments.

The school had no website at all. Admissions inquiries came entirely through word of mouth, and the school had no way to share its curriculum, showcase achievements, or communicate with prospective parents online.

They needed a website that would make parents feel confident, informed, and excited about enrolling — before they'd even visited the school in person.

What We Built

Features That Win Enrollments
