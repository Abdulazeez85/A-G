The Challenge

A Great Restaurant With Zero Online Presence

Lagos Spice Kitchen had loyal regulars and outstanding food — but no website, no online menu, and no way for new customers to find them. Business was heavily word-of-mouth and not growing.

They needed a website that felt as premium as their food: rich visuals, an easy-to-navigate menu, and a frictionless way for customers to place orders via WhatsApp.

The site also needed to load fast on mobile data connections and be easy for the owner to update without technical knowledge.

What We Built

Features That Drive Bookings

🍽️

Digital Menu System

Categorized menu with photos, descriptions, and prices. Easy to browse on any device.

📱

WhatsApp Order Integration

One-click order button pre-fills a WhatsApp message with the customer's chosen dishes.

📸

Food Photo Gallery

Rich visual gallery showcasing signature dishes and the restaurant ambience.

📅

Table Reservation Form

Simple booking form that sends reservation details directly to the owner's WhatsApp.

📍

Location & Hours

Clear display of location, opening hours, and directions with Google Maps link.

Lightning Fast Load

Optimized images and minimal JavaScript ensure fast loading even on slow mobile data.
