FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does a website take to build? +
Most standard websites are delivered within 7–14 business days. Complex projects with custom features may take 2–4 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline before we start.
Do you offer hosting and domain setup? +
Yes! We handle everything — domain registration, hosting setup, SSL certificate, and email configuration. You can also use your existing hosting if preferred.
Will my website be mobile responsive? +
Absolutely. Every website we build is fully responsive and tested on multiple devices and screen sizes. Mobile-first design is our standard, not an add-on.
Can you redesign my existing website? +
Yes. We offer complete website redesigns. We'll review your current site, understand what's working, and build you something significantly better while preserving your SEO value.
Do you offer SEO services? +
All our websites include basic on-page SEO — proper meta tags, semantic HTML, image optimization, and fast loading speeds. Extended SEO packages are available as an add-on.
What are your payment terms? +
We typically require 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. We accept bank transfer and mobile payments. Milestone payments are available for larger projects.
